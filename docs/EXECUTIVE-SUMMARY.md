## RISK ASSESSMENT & MITIGATION

### **üî¥ HIGH RISK AREAS**

**Risk 1: Homepage Authority Loss**
- **Probability**: Low (same URL)
- **Impact**: High (main ranking page)
- **Mitigation**: Preserve URL, expand content, maintain NAP, implement LocalBusiness schema

**Risk 2: Service Page Rankings Drop**
- **Probability**: Low (URLs preserved)
- **Impact**: High (money pages)
- **Mitigation**: 1:1 URL mapping, content expansion not replacement, 301 redirects for any changes

**Risk 3: Google My Business Disconnect**
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: High (local SEO)
- **Mitigation**: Update GMB website URL immediately post-launch, verify ownership

### **üü¢ LOW RISK AREAS**

**Risk 4: New Page Indexing Issues**
- **Probability**: Low
- **Impact**: Low (new content)
- **Mitigation**: Submit sitemap, request indexing via GSC
